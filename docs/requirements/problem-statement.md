# Problem Statement

Traditional Damascene handcrafted products are often presented through unstructured, commercial platforms that fail to reflect their cultural, artistic, and heritage value.

Current e-commerce solutions focus on speed and mass selling, which conflicts with the calm, museum-like experience required for heritage products.

The problem addressed by this project is how to design and implement a digital platform that:
- Preserves cultural authenticity
- Presents products as art objects rather than commodities
- Scales technically without sacrificing identity
